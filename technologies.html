Cisco Systems Ecosystem

The foundation of secure, scalable, and resilient enterprise networks.

Cisco continues to lead the global networking industry by delivering proven solutions across campus, WAN, data center, and security domains. At NetKode, we build Cisco-based infrastructures that combine rock-solid reliability with modern automation and programmability.

Our Cisco architectures are designed to support large user populations, high-performance applications, and distributed environments while maintaining centralized visibility and policy enforcement. We help organizations transition from traditional networking models to intent-based networking, where business requirements are translated directly into automated network policies.

Using platforms such as Cisco DNA Center, ACI, and ISE, we enable consistent configuration, continuous compliance, and simplified operations across the entire enterprise.

Core Capabilities

  • Catalyst 9000 switching & SD-Access
  • Nexus 9000, VXLAN EVPN & Cisco ACI
  • Cisco ISE for Zero Trust access
  • Firepower NGFW & secure segmentation

Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Cloud-native networking built for scale, automation, and security.

AWS provides a highly flexible networking foundation designed for rapid innovation and massive scalability. We architect Virtual Private Clouds (VPCs) that support complex enterprise workloads while maintaining strong isolation between environments.

Our AWS designs use Transit Gateways, centralized firewalls, and Infrastructure-as-Code to deliver consistent routing, inspection, and governance across multiple accounts and regions. This allows application teams to move faster while security teams retain full visibility and control.

From hybrid connectivity to cloud-native architectures, we ensure AWS networks remain resilient, secure, and cost-optimized.

  • VPC, subnet & route table design
  • Transit Gateway & multi-account routing
  • Direct Connect & Site-to-Site VPN
  • ALB/NLB & Route 53 DNS

Linux & Open Source Networking

The backbone of automation, observability, and network programmability.

Linux plays a critical role in modern network automation and telemetry platforms. We use Linux-based systems as control planes for managing configurations, collecting metrics, and orchestrating CI/CD-driven infrastructure changes.

By leveraging open-source technologies, organizations gain flexibility, transparency, and vendor independence. Our solutions integrate automation frameworks, container platforms, and routing stacks to enable highly customizable networking environments.

  • Ansible & Python automation
  • Docker & Kubernetes networking
  • FRRouting, DNS & monitoring stacks

SD-WAN Platforms

Intelligent WAN transformation with application-aware routing.

SD-WAN replaces rigid MPLS architectures with software-defined networks that dynamically optimize traffic based on application requirements. We design SD-WAN solutions that prioritize business-critical applications while reducing connectivity costs.

Our SD-WAN deployments integrate cloud security and SASE platforms, providing consistent policy enforcement across branch offices, data centers, and remote users. Zero-touch provisioning enables rapid branch onboarding with minimal operational effort.

  • Cisco Viptela & Meraki SD-WAN
  • Zero-touch branch provisioning
  • Cloud security & SASE integration
